**百年義大利黃金之聲 ✕ 范．克萊本大賽最年輕金獎得主**

**樂迷年度必聽的金色交響盛宴，再現道地義式聲響美學！**

  

義大利國寶級百年天團睽違七年再度來臺！以音樂的主保聖人為名，聖西西里亞管絃樂團擁有輝煌歷史，是義大利首支著重交響樂的管絃樂團。他們以溫暖熱情的音色、豐沛細膩的詮釋，完美展現道地義式風采，被樂迷稱為「義大利的黃金之聲」！

  

由新任音樂總監、被譽為「指揮奇才」的丹尼爾．哈丁領軍，聖西西里亞管絃樂團本次來臺，將以威爾第《西西里晚禱》序曲展現本色，並用義式美學渲染布拉姆斯充滿田園氣息的第二號交響曲。范．克萊本大賽最年輕的金獎得主任奫燦，更將攜手演出拉威爾節奏多變的G大調鋼琴協奏曲，絕對是今年必聽的經典饗宴！

  

Italy's treasured century-old orchestra is returning to Taiwan after seven
years. Named after the patron saint of music, the Orchestra dell'Accademia
Nazionale di Santa Cecilia holds a distinguished place in history as Italy's
first orchestra dedicated to symphonic repertoire. Renowned for its warm,
passionate tone and richly nuanced interpretations, the ensemble embodies the
true spirit of Italian artistry—earning it the title "the golden voice of
Italy" among music lovers.

  
Led by its new music director Daniel Harding, the orchestra will show its
distinctive quality with the overture to Verdi's _Sicilian Vespers_ and infuse
its Italian character into Brahm's _Symphony No. 2_. In addition, the youngest
ever winner of the Van Cliburn International Piano Competition, Yunchan Lim,
will join the orchestra to perform Ravel's _Piano Concerto in G Major_. With
such a dynamic program, this is sure to be one of the most popular concerts of
the season.

聖西西里亞管絃樂團 Orchestra dell'Accademia Nazionale di Santa Cecilia

  

聖西西里亞管絃樂團是義大利首支專注於交響曲目演出的樂團，並曾首演二十世紀多部重要作品，如雷史畢基的《羅馬之泉》與《羅馬之松》。自1908年以來，許多傳奇音樂家皆曾登上該團的指揮台，包括馬勒、理察．史特勞斯、史特拉汶斯基、西貝流士、辛德密特、托斯卡尼尼、福特萬格勒、德．薩巴塔、克萊巴父子、蕭提、孟根堡、卡拉揚、史托科夫斯基、萊納、布魯諾．華特、傑利畢達克、馬述爾、布隆斯泰特、阿巴多、杜達美與佩特連科等。其常任指揮曾包括莫利納里、費拉拉、普雷維塔利、馬克維契、席佩斯、辛諾波里、丹尼爾．加蒂、鄭明勳，以及安東尼奧．帕帕諾爵士。伯恩斯坦曾於1983年至1990年間擔任榮譽主席。自2024/25樂季起，新任音樂總監為丹尼爾．哈丁。

  

該團曾受邀參與世界重要音樂節，如倫敦逍遙音樂節、琉森音樂節、聖彼得堡音樂節與薩爾茲堡音樂節，並曾在多個國際頂級音樂廳演出，包括柏林愛樂廳、德勒斯登森柏歌劇院、漢堡易北愛樂廳、維也納金色大廳、阿姆斯特丹音樂廳、米蘭史卡拉歌劇院、東京三得利音樂廳、布宜諾斯艾利斯科隆劇院與紐約卡內基音樂廳。2024年，該團與合唱團更以駐團身分參與享有盛名的薩爾茲堡復活節音樂節。

  

在帕帕諾指揮下，樂團近年積極錄音並屢獲國際殊榮，代表錄音包括與阿格麗希合作的聖桑《動物狂歡節》、伯恩斯坦全套交響曲、理察．史特勞斯《英雄的生涯》、羅西尼《光榮彌撒》、威爾第《阿伊達》與《奧賽羅》（與尤納斯．考夫曼合作）、《杜蘭朵》（與桑德拉．雷瓦諾夫斯基與尤納斯．考夫曼合作）。

  

隨著丹尼爾．哈丁的上任，樂團將展開與德意志留聲機的新錄音計畫，首張專輯預計於2024年10月現場錄製，內容為普契尼的《托斯卡》。此外，哈丁亦帶領樂團展開歐洲重要巡演，足跡遍及巴黎、盧森堡、安特衛普與維也納。

  

The Orchestra dell'Accademia Nazionale di Santa Cecilia was the first
orchestra in Italy to devote itself exclusively to the symphonic repertoire,
premiering 20th-century masterpieces such as Ottorino Respighi's _Fountains of
Rome_ and _Pines of Rome_. Since 1908, its podium has been visited by the
likes of Mahler, R. Strauss, Stravinsky, Sibelius, Hindemith, Toscanini,
Furtwängler, De Sabata, Erich and Carlos Kleiber, Solti, Mengelberg, Karajan,
Stokowski, Reiner, Bruno Walter, Celibidache, Furtwängler, Masur, Blomstedt,
Abbado, Dudamel, and Kirill Petrenko. Bernardino Molinari, Franco Ferrara,
Fernando Previtali, Igor Markevitch, Thomas Schippers, Giuseppe Sinopoli,
Daniele Gatti, Myung-Whun Chung, and Sir Antonio Pappano (2005-2023) have been
its permanent conductors. Beginning with the 2024-25 season, the new Music
Director is Daniel Harding. Leonard Bernstein, was its Honorary President from
1983 to 1990.

  

The Orchestra has appeared at the foremost international festivals – the Proms
in London and the Lucerne, Saint Petersburg, and Salzburg Festivals – and in
some of the most prestigious concert venues, including Berliner Philharmonie,
Semperoper in Dresden, Elbphilharmonie in Hamburg, Musikverein in Vienna,
Concertgebouw in Amsterdam, La Scala in Milan, Tokyo's Suntory Hall, Teatro
Colón in Buenos Aires, and Carnegie Hall in New York. In 2024, the Orchestra
and Chorus were resident guests at the prestigious Salzburg Easter Festival.

  

The intense recording activity with Pappano in recent years garnered
prestigious international honours. Recordings include _The Carnival of the
Animals_ by Saint-Saëns with Martha Argerich, Bernstein's Complete Symphonies,
Richard Strauss's _Ein Heldenleben_ , Rossini's _Messa di Gloria_ , Verdi's
_Aida_ and _Otello_ (with Jonas Kaufmann), _Turandot_ with Sondra Radvanovsky
and Jonas Kaufmann, and some recitals with Anna Netrebko. Warner Classics has
published a 27-CD box set containing a large portion of the pieces from the
religious and symphonic repertoire recorded under Pappano's baton by the
Orchestra and Chorus over the past 18 years.

  

With Harding, recordings will start on a new course with the Deutsche
Grammophon label. The first will be dedicated to Puccini's Tosca, recorded
live in October 2024. Also with Harding, the Orchestra embarked on a major
Europe tour that visited Paris, Luxembourg, Antwerp, and Vienna.

丹尼爾．哈丁 Daniel Harding

  

丹尼爾．哈丁榮獲大英帝國司令勳章（CBE），現任瑞典廣播交響樂團的音樂與藝術總監，本樂季為他與該團合作的第十八年。同時，他也將展開新的旅程，擔任聖西西里亞管絃樂團的新任音樂總監。他亦為馬勒室內樂團的榮譽指揮，與該團合作已超過二十年。

  

哈丁經常受邀與世界頂尖樂團合作，包括維也納愛樂、柏林愛樂、皇家阿姆斯特丹大會堂管弦樂團、巴伐利亞廣播交響樂團、德勒斯登國家管弦樂團、倫敦交響樂團與米蘭斯卡拉愛樂樂團。在美國，他曾與波士頓交響樂團、克里夫蘭管弦樂團、芝加哥交響樂團、洛杉磯愛樂、紐約愛樂及舊金山交響樂團合作演出。

  

作為一位備受推崇的歌劇指揮，哈丁曾於米蘭史卡拉歌劇院、維也納國立歌劇院、倫敦皇家歌劇院科芬園，以及普羅旺斯艾克斯與薩爾茲堡音樂節指揮備受好評的製作。

  

他同時也是一位具備資格的飛行員。

  

Daniel Harding CBE is Music and Artistic Director of the Swedish Radio
Symphony Orchestra, with whom this season he celebrates an 18-year
anniversary, marking the end of his tenure with the Orchestra. During the same
season, he embarks on a new journey as Music Director of the Accademia
Nazionale di Santa Cecilia. He is Conductor Laureate of the Mahler Chamber
Orchestra, with whom he has worked for over 20 years. Earlier this year, he
took up the position of Music Director of the Youth Music Culture, The Greater
Bay Area.

  

He is a regular visitor to the world's foremost orchestras, including the
Wiener Philharmoniker, Berliner Philharmoniker, Royal Concertgebouw Orchestra,
Symphonieorchester des Bayerischen Rundfunks, Staatskapelle Dresden, London
Symphony Orchestra and the Orchestra Filarmonica della Scala. In the US, he
has appeared with the Boston Symphony Orchestra, The Cleveland Orchestra,
Chicago Symphony Orchestra, Los Angeles Philharmonic, New York Philharmonic
and the San Francisco Symphony.

  

A renowned opera conductor, he has led critically acclaimed productions at the
Teatro alla Scala Milan, Wiener Staatsoper, Royal Opera House, Covent Garden
and at the Aix-en-Provence and Salzburg Festivals.

  

He is a qualified airline pilot.

任奫燦 Yunchan Lim

> 「百萬中選一的天才」—— _《達拉斯晨報》_  
>  “A one-in-a-million talent.” – Dallas Morning News  
>

2022年，年僅十八歲的任奫燦在范．克萊本國際鋼琴大賽中勇奪金牌，成為該賽事史上最年輕的冠軍，自此迅速躍升為國際巨星。他的演奏展現出「魔幻般的能力」與「與生俱來、直覺性的特質」（La
Scena），令全球樂迷驚嘆不已。決賽的指揮艾索普表示：「任奫燦是那種極為罕見的藝術家，能將深刻的音樂性與驚人的技巧自然地結合在一起。」任奫燦詮釋拉赫曼尼諾夫第三號鋼琴協奏曲的影片在賽後幾天內迅速登上
YouTube 全球熱搜，目前已成為該曲目最多人觀看的版本，點閱數突破1,500萬次。《紐約時報》更將該演出列為「2022十大古典音樂演出」之一。

  

在奪冠後的幾年間，任奫燦陸續與紐約、洛杉磯、慕尼黑、首爾等愛樂交響樂團合作，並與芝加哥、琉森、BBC、波士頓與東京交響樂團等展開合作，皆大獲成功。他也於多個重要舞台舉辦獨奏會，包括卡內基音樂廳、維爾比耶音樂節、威格摩音樂廳、阿姆斯特丹音樂廳與東京三得利音樂廳等。

  

在2024/25樂季，任奫燦將首次與華盛頓國家交響樂團、倫敦交響樂團、英國皇家愛樂、奧地利維也納廣播交響樂團、德國柏林廣播交響樂團與WDR西德廣播交響樂團合作演出，並將重返紐約愛樂、琉森交響樂團與巴黎管弦樂團。此外，他也將首次於甘迺迪中心舉辦獨奏會，並重返卡內基音樂廳。

  

Since becoming the youngest person to ever win gold at the Van Cliburn
International Piano Competition at the age of 18 in 2022, Yunchan Lim's ascent
to international stardom has been meteoric. His performances showcase a
"magical ability" and a "natural, instinctive quality" (La Scena) that
astounds listeners around the world. Marin Alsop, who conducted the 2022
Cliburn Final said, "Yunchan is that rare artist who brings profound
musicality and prodigious technique organically together."

  

In the years following his Cliburn win, Yunchan made successful orchestral
debuts with the New York, Los Angeles, Munich, and Seoul Philharmonic
orchestras, as well as Chicago, Lucerne, BBC, Boston, and Tokyo Symphony
orchestras among others. Recital appearances include performances at Carnegie
Hall, Verbier Festival, the Wigmore Hall, Het Concertgebouw, and Suntory Hall,
among other major stages.

  

Lim's 2024/25 season highlights include orchestral debuts with Washington
National Symphony, London Symphony, Royal Philharmonic, Vienna Radio Symphony,
Berlin Radio Symphony, and WDR Symphony Orchestras, as well as returning to
New York Philharmonic, Lucerne Symphony Orchestra, and Orchestra de Paris.
This season will also see his recital debut at the Kennedy Center, and a
return to Carnegie Hall.
