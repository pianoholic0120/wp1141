##  節目介紹

  

  

  

《Bed Trip》是一場從「失眠」展開的旅程。

失眠，不只是夜不能寐，而是一種清醒過頭的狀態—身體早已疲憊，意識卻被困在壓力與未解的問題中，不肯熄燈。

人們總會嘗試一種約定俗成的入睡儀式—數羊。那是一種看似天真的自我催眠，卻往往徒勞。或許，問題不在方法，而在於你遇見了一隻「卡住的羊」—一隻遲遲不肯跳過去的羊。牠一旦停下，睡意便全數崩塌。

在這個作品中，羊不再是助眠的可愛符號，而是焦慮的化身：牠們被數、被跳、被馴服，如同現代社會對女性的期待—順從、柔軟、無礙於群體節奏，永不「卡住」。而那隻拒絕跳的羊，則是身體的抗議，是內心尚未被容許存在的聲音。

**  
**

《Bed Trip》邀請觀眾去凝視這隻羊——牠是否一定要跳過去？還是，我們也可以選擇和牠並肩坐下？

**  
**

_Bed Trip_ is a journey that begins with insomnia.

Insomnia is not merely the inability to sleep, but a state of being overly
awake—when the body is already exhausted, yet the mind remains trapped in
pressures and unresolved questions, refusing to switch off.

People often resort to a customary ritual of sleep—counting sheep. It is a
seemingly innocent act of self-hypnosis, but more often than not, in vain.
Perhaps the problem is not the method, but the moment you encounter a 'stuck
sheep'—one that simply refuses to leap across. Once it stops, the entire
weight of sleep collapses.

In this work, the sheep is no longer a cute symbol of slumber, but the
embodiment of anxiety: they are counted, they leap, they are tamed—just as
modern society expects women to be obedient, gentle, and untroubled in rhythm
with the group, never allowed to ‘get stuck.’

That sheep which refuses to leap is the protest of the body, the voice within
that has not yet been permitted to exist.

**  
**

_Bed Trip_ invites the audience to confront the sheep — does it have to leap,
or might we simply sit with it?

**團隊介紹**

**Eye Catching Circus****創造焦點**

創造焦點馬戲團成立於2015年，致力於融合雜技、馬戲與劇場，創新詮釋當代馬戲藝術。團隊擁有十餘年專業學習資歷，屢獲殊榮，包括臺中市傑出演藝團隊（2018-2020、2023）及
Taiwan Top 演藝團隊（2024-2025）。十年來，已累積豐富國內外演出經驗，製作九部當代馬戲作品，如《控制狂 Control+》、《保持聯繫
Keep in
touch!》、《日和盛綻》等，並參與各大藝術節與文化活動。創造焦點足跡遍及高雄衛武營、臺灣燈會、臺北白晝之夜等，並代表臺灣登上國際舞臺，包括愛丁堡藝穗節（2023）、阿德雷德藝穗節、亞維儂藝術節、巴黎文化奧運臺灣館、捷克
Letní Letná 藝術節（2024）。未來，團隊期望深化國際交流，將所學帶回臺灣，推動當代馬戲美學，讓世界看見臺灣馬戲的獨特魅力。

  

Founded in 2015, the troupe was honored as Outstanding Performing Arts Group
by Taichung City for the years 2018-2020 and again in 2023. They were also
recognized as a Taiwan Top Performing Arts Group in 2024-2025. With over a
decade of professional experience, the troupe combines innovation with
tradition, integrating professional stunts and theatrical skills. This
amalgamation has garnered them extensive performance experience both
domestically and internationally. They are dedicated to applying contemporary
perspectives and exploring innovative styles to blend acrobatics and circus
acts, offering fresh interpretations of contemporary circus art and its
essence. Moreover, the troupe is steadfast in their mission to promote and
advance Taiwan's modern circus scene. In 2022, Eye Catching Circus received an
invitation from the Phare Ponleu Selpak circus school to take part in the Tini
Tinou International Circus Festival in Cambodia. In 2023, the troupe was
invited to the Focasa Circus Festival and represented Taiwan in the Edinburgh
Festival Fringe. In 2024, the troupe already took part in the Adelaide Fringe
in Australia and it will represent Taiwan at the Festival Off Avignon in
France and participate in the Taiwanese representative performance team at the
Cultural Olympics in Paris, France. Additionally, they have been invited to
perform at the International Festival of Contemporary Circus and Theatre Letní
Letná in Prague, Czech Republic.

  

In the future, we also hope to exchange and learn with another companys from
various countries, bring experience and creative energy back to Taiwan, create
a new focus of contemporary circus, and let more people see the contemporary
circus aesthetics that balance traditional culture and contemporary
performances.

  

**YUCK Circus**

YUCK 是一家來自西澳的製作公司，致力於推動女性在現場表演中的發聲。無論是在舞台上、技術台後，還是在創意或管理角色中，YUCK
的宗旨是讓女性及女性認同者有機會向世界展示她們的才華。YUCK
的成員來自澳洲四面八方的偏遠、孤立小鎮，我們熱衷於分享作品並強化社區聯繫。這是一支由精英女馬戲演員組成的團隊，準備顛覆雙重標準，並以藝術之名勇敢挑戰。我們不只是輕描淡寫地談論女性議題——我們是字面意義上的「拋起」女性。隨著我們原創作品的成功，我們欣喜地擴展至創作更多新作品。目前，我們已有五個獨立表演，充滿了我們獨特的幽默風格和精彩的高空雜技，並在國內外巡演，獲得了數十項大獎，讓成千上萬的觀眾為之驚嘆。

  

A crew of elite acrobatic gals, ready to flip off double-standards and kick
art in the face. We're not lightly throwing around women's issues – we're
literally throwing women.

With the success of our original production, we're stoked to have grown to
produce new work.

We've now got 5 independent shows chokkas with our brand of larrikin humour
and high-flying acrobatics, touring nationally and internationally, picking up
dozens of awards, and thrilling thousands from the public along the way.
