**華爾街日報「最天賦異稟的鋼琴家」**

**以琴鍵詮釋青春的無懼、浪漫與夢想！**

  

他的琴聲兼具優雅、力量與洞見——挪威鋼琴家萊夫．奧維．安斯涅，以精湛技巧和深刻詮釋廣受樂迷愛戴。華爾街日報稱他是「同世代中最天賦異稟的鋼琴家」，因為雖非大賽出身，安斯涅卻靠著一場場極富巧思的演奏、一張張選曲精妙的專輯，逐步成就今日大師地位！

  

睽違十年再度來臺，安斯涅將獻上葛利格、蕭邦與舒曼年輕時的鋼琴作品。在繽紛的音色中，他藉舒曼的《嘉年華》遊歷年輕作曲家筆下的眾生相，並以蕭邦的《前奏曲》展現抒情與戲劇張力，邀您一起歌詠生命脈動的喜悅。

  

Hailed by the _New York Times_ as "a pianist of magisterial elegance, power,
and insight," Leif Ove Andsnes is adored by music lovers for his impeccable
technique and profound interpretations. Called "one of the most gifted
musicians of his generation" by the _Wall Street Journal_ , the Norwegian
pianist earned his stellar reputation gradually through ingeniously programmed
concerts and exquisitely crafted albums, rather than through major
competitions.

  
Returning to Taiwan after 10 years, Leif Ove Andsnes presents piano works from
the youthful years of Grieg, Chopin, and Schumann. Through the colorful
soundscapes of Schumann's Carnaval, he journeys into the vivid characters
imagined by a young composer, and with Chopin's Preludes he reveals both
lyricism and dramatic intensity—inviting you to share in the joy of life's
vibrant pulse.

✦ 鋼琴家安斯涅為此提出以下說明

​

經過深思熟慮後，我決定將此次演出的曲目做一些調整，能更貼近當下的自己。謹讓我向各位介紹新的曲目安排。

​

葛利格的鋼琴奏鳴曲是他在21歲時創作的作品，深受舒曼鋼琴風格影響，洋溢著青春的氣息。這部作品也成為接續演出的舒曼《嘉年華》的理想前奏。無論是葛利格還是舒曼，這兩部作品都展現出年輕人特有的熱情與征服世界的渴望。

​

而舒曼的《嘉年華》色彩繽紛、充滿活力，帶著戲劇性的張力。它一方面與蕭邦《前奏曲》相呼應，另一方面又形成鮮明對比。兩者同樣展現了戲劇性與抒情、華麗的技巧交織其中。但《嘉年華》屬於標題音樂，描繪了真實人物（其中包括蕭邦）及舒曼虛構的「大衛同盟」；蕭邦的作品則是絕對音樂，沒有特定形象，卻充滿詩意，有時甚至喚起對人物或景象的聯想。

​

這兩部作品都展現了對鋼琴與音樂語言的巧妙運用，並且創作時代相近——《嘉年華》於1835年完成，《前奏曲》則在隨後三、四年間陸續誕生。那確實是鋼琴音樂史上的輝煌年代！

  

希望你們會喜歡這樣的巧思。我們音樂會現場見！

​



—— 萊夫．奧維．安斯涅

**萊夫．奧維．安斯涅  Leif Ove Andsnes**

> 「一位擁有帝王般優雅、力度與深刻見解的鋼琴家。」—— 紐約時報  
> "A pianist of magisterial elegance, power, and insight."—— _New York Times_

萊夫．奧維．安斯涅以其強大的技巧與深刻的詮釋力享譽國際，常在世界各大音樂廳與頂尖樂團合作演出協奏曲與獨奏會，並累積了備受推崇、種類多元的錄音作品。身為一位熱衷室內樂的音樂家，他是羅森達爾室內樂節的創辦人兼藝術總監，並曾擔任挪威利瑟室內樂節近二十年的共同藝術總監，以及2012年美國加州奧海音樂節的音樂總監。他於2013年7月入選《留聲機》名人堂，並獲頒卑爾根大學、奧斯陸大學與紐約茱莉亞音樂學院的名譽博士學位。

  

安斯涅的錄音作品超過50張，涵蓋獨奏、室內樂與協奏曲類型，曲目橫跨巴洛克至當代，許多皆為暢銷專輯。他曾11度獲葛萊美獎提名，並獲得七座《留聲機》大獎。他與EMI錄製的同鄉作曲家葛利格作品備受讚譽；他與楊頌斯及柏林愛樂合作的《鋼琴協奏曲》獲《紐約時報》選為2004年「年度最佳專輯」，《企鵝唱片指南》給予「三星帶花」最高榮譽；他錄製的莫札特第9與18號鋼琴協奏曲亦被《紐約時報》選為「年度最佳專輯」，並獲《企鵝唱片指南》「三星帶花」榮譽。他與安東尼奧．帕帕諾及柏林愛樂錄製的拉赫曼尼諾夫第一與第二號協奏曲也贏得《留聲機》大獎。除了「貝多芬之旅」與「莫札特1785/86」之外，他近期的專輯包括德弗札克《Poetic
Tone Pictures》、蕭邦《敘事曲與夜曲》與暢銷專輯《西貝流士》；他亦與鋼琴家馬克-
安卓．漢莫林合作灌錄《春之祭》雙鋼琴版本，與男中音馬蒂亞斯．葛納錄製舒曼《藝術歌曲集》，這兩張專輯皆獲葛萊美獎提名。

  

安斯涅曾獲挪威國王頒授聖奧拉夫勳章指揮官勳位，並於2007年獲挪威國會議員頒發皮爾金獎（Peer Gynt
Prize），表揚他在政治、體育與文化領域的傑出貢獻。他是首位擔任紐約卡內基音樂廳「觀點系列」策展人的斯堪地那維亞音樂家，也曾是倫敦交響樂團藝術家肖像系列主角。此外，他曾任柏林愛樂、紐約愛樂與瑞典哥德堡交響樂團駐團藝術家。他亦獲皇家愛樂協會器樂演奏家獎與吉爾摩藝術家獎（Gilmore
Artist Award），並於2005年被《浮華世界》雜誌評為「最傑出人物」之一。

  

With his commanding technique and searching interpretations, the celebrated
Norwegian pianist Leif Ove Andsnes has won acclaim worldwide, playing
concertos and recitals in the world's leading concert halls and with its
foremost orchestras, while building an esteemed and extensive discography. An
avid chamber musician, he is the founding director of the Rosendal Chamber
Music Festival, was co-artistic director of the Risør Festival of Chamber
Music for nearly two decades, and served as music director of California's
Ojai Music Festival in 2012. He was inducted into the Gramophone Hall of Fame
in July 2013, and has received honorary doctorates from Norway's Universities
of Bergen and Oslo and New York's Juilliard School.

  

Andsnes's discography comprises more than 50 titles – solo, chamber, and
concerto releases, many of them bestsellers – spanning repertoire from the
Baroque to the present day. He has been nominated for eleven Grammys and his
many international prizes include seven Gramophone Awards. His EMI Classics
recordings of the music of his compatriot Edvard Grieg have been especially
celebrated: the _New York Times_ named Andsnes's 2004 recording of Grieg's
Piano Concerto with Mariss Jansons and the Berlin Philharmonic a "Best CD of
the Year," the _Penguin Guide_ awarded it a coveted "Rosette," and both that
album and his disc of Grieg's Lyric Pieces won Gramophone Awards. His
recording of Mozart's Piano Concertos Nos. 9 and 18 was another _New York
Times_ "Best of the Year" and _Penguin Guide_ "Rosette" honoree. He won yet
another Gramophone Award for Rachmaninov's Piano Concertos Nos. 1 and 2 with
Antonio Pappano and the Berlin Philharmonic. In addition to The Beethoven
Journey and MM 1785/86, his recent Sony Classical releases include Dvořák's
unjustly neglected piano cycle _Poetic Tone Pictures_ , _Chopin: Ballades &
Nocturnes_, and the Billboard best-selling Sibelius, all recorded for Sony;
_Stravinsky: The Rite of Spring & other works_ for two pianos four hands,
recorded with Marc-André Hamelin for Hyperion; and _Schumann: Liederkreis &
Kernerlieder_, recorded with Matthias Goerne for Harmonia Mundi. Both the
Hamelin and Goerne collaborations were nominated for Grammy Awards.

  

Andsnes has received Norway's distinguished honor, Commander of the Royal
Norwegian Order of St. Olav, and in 2007, he received the prestigious Peer
Gynt Prize, awarded by members of parliament to honor prominent Norwegians for
their achievements in politics, sports, and culture. In 2004-05, he became the
youngest musician (and first Scandinavian) to curate Carnegie Hall's
"Perspectives" series, and in 2015-16 he was the subject of the London
Symphony Orchestra's Artist Portrait Series. Having been 2010-11 Pianist-in-
Residence of the Berlin Philharmonic, he went on to serve as 2017-18 Artist-
in-Residence of the New York Philharmonic and 2019-20 Artist-in-Residence of
Sweden's Gothenburg Symphony. The recipient of both the Royal Philharmonic
Society's Instrumentalist Award and the Gilmore Artist Award, Andsnes was
named one of the "Best of the Best" by _Vanity Fair_ in 2005.
