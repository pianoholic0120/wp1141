##  節目介紹

  

  

**「極其自在，無需擔心技術上的困難，他讓鋼琴歌唱！」——萊比錫人民報**

**鋼琴巨星基里爾·格斯坦首度來臺獨奏會！**  

  

以驚人技巧與深邃樂思活耀於國際樂壇的鋼琴巨星——基里爾·格斯坦，即將來臺舉辦首場獨奏會！
2001年榮獲魯賓斯坦鋼琴大賽首獎便嶄露頭角，曾與維也納愛樂、柏林愛樂、阿姆斯特丹皇家大會堂管絃樂團、巴伐利亞廣播交響樂團等國際頂尖樂團合作。專輯曾獲回聲獎、三項葛萊美獎提名及留聲機獎；2024年最新專輯《Music
in Time of
War》獲得《衛報》四星讚譽，《紐約古典音樂評論》更盛讚他技藝精湛、張弛有度，同時展現細膩內斂的風範。此次演出將與觀眾一同品味浪漫時期布拉姆斯與李斯特經典作品的深厚內涵！

  

**Gloriously free and unfazed by technical difficulties, he made the piano
sing – Leipziger Volkszeitung**

**Internationally acclaimed pianist Gerstein's first recital in Taiwan**

  

Internationally acclaimed for his stellar technical skills and thoughtful
musicianship, the piano superstar Kirill Gerstein is performing in Taiwan for
his first recital. Gerstein rose to prominence when he won the Arthur
Rubinstein International Piano Master Competition in 2001. Since then, he has
performed with top international orchestras including the Vienna Philharmonic,
Berlin Philharmonic, Royal Concertgebouw Orchestra, and Bavarian Radio
Symphony Orchestra. His album recordings have been recognized with Echo
Klassik and Gramophone awards, as well as three Grammy Award nominations. His
newest album released in 2024, Music in Time of War, received a four-star
rating from The Guardian. In addition, the New York Classical Review has
praised his virtuosity and dexterity, saying he makes even the most
technically difficult pieces sound easy. His recital will feature pieces by
Brahms and Liszt, with his unparalleled technical dexterity bringing out the
intricacy of these classic works.

![](https://s3.resource.opentix.life/upload/program/17509090280386xm5SFrqhC.jpeg)  

基里爾．格斯坦以豐沛的好奇心、無限的創造力與耀眼的技巧，成為當代最具影響力的鋼琴家之一。他不僅是獨具慧眼的演奏者，更身兼策展人、教育者、音樂推動者，橫跨古典、當代、爵士乃至歌廳音樂，持續開拓音樂的邊界，並與世界各大樂團、指揮、作曲家與藝術節建立深厚合作關係。
近年重要合作計畫包括擔任巴伐利亞廣播交響樂團駐團藝術家、倫敦交響樂團焦點音樂家、普羅旺斯艾克斯音樂節駐團藝術家，以及於倫敦維格摩音樂廳策畫「布梭尼與他的世界」系列音樂會。他亦與柏林愛樂與基里爾．佩特連科合作推出為紀念拉赫曼尼諾夫誕辰150周年的專輯。
格斯坦在數位與影音平台亦極具創意，與 Apple
Music、Myrios、DG、DECCA、柏林愛樂自有品牌合作發行專輯。其最新製作《戰時之聲》將德布西晚期鋼琴作品與亞美尼亞作曲家科米塔斯之作並置，並以結合書籍、影像與評論的新型態專輯形式發行。
作為當代音樂的積極推動者，格斯坦長期委託並首演新作，合作對象包括蒂莫·安德絲、奇克·柯瑞亞、亞歷山大·高爾、奧利佛·克努森與布拉德·梅爾道等。自 2019
年世界首演湯瑪斯．阿戴斯鋼琴協奏曲以來，格斯坦已在三大洲與二十個樂團合作超過五十場演出。近期他亦與卑爾根愛樂與指揮愛德華．加德納合作，為 ECM
錄製湯瑪斯·拉歇爾鋼琴協奏曲，並將於 2025/26 樂季首演西班牙作曲家法蘭斯高·科爾·加西亞為其創作的新協奏曲。
格斯坦同時致力於教學與知識傳遞，現任柏林漢斯·艾斯勒音樂院鋼琴教授，並為克隆貝格學院師資成員。他於克隆貝格策劃的免費線上講座系列，邀請當代重要藝術思想家對談，至今累計超過十五萬名觀眾。格斯坦亦於韋爾比耶音樂節學院與
IMS Prussia Cove 擔任教師。

  

Fascination for musical discovery combined with boundless curiosity,
imagination, and virtuosity have established Kirill Gerstein as one of today's
most prolific and compelling performers. Gerstein is a searching artist. As a
pianist, curator, educator, musical leader, and artistic collaborator, his
exploration of resonant themes across a vast spectrum of repertoire - from
Baroque suites and Classical concerti to contemporary creations, jazz, and
cabaret – has nourished relationships with many of the world's leading
orchestras, conductors, instrumentalists, singers, composers, festivals,
recording labels, and media platforms. Most recently, Gerstein was Artist-in-
Residence with the Bavarian Radio Symphony Orchestra, Spotlight Artist with
the London Symphony Orchestra, Resident Artist at the Festival Aix-en-
Provence, and curated a three-part “Busoni and his World” concert series at
London's Wigmore Hall. He also released an acclaimed album with the Berliner
Philharmoniker and Kirill Petrenko celebrating Rachmaninov's 150th
anniversary. Media projects, broadcasts, and digital innovation represent an
integral part of Gerstein's creativity. He has recorded for Platoon/Apple
Music, myrios, Deutsche Grammophon, DECCA, Berliner Philharmoniker Recordings.
Gerstein's latest media project entitled “Music in Time of War”, pairs late
piano works by Claude Debussy with pieces by Armenian priest, musicologist,
and composer Vardapet Komitas. Expanding the traditional album concept, the
recording is integrated into a hardcover book containing a wealth of
documentary images and specially commissioned original scholarship. A true
champion of music of our time, Gerstein has commissioned and premièred new
works by Timo Andres, Chick Corea, Alexander Goehr, Oliver Knussen and Brad
Mehldau, among others. Since giving the world première of Thomas Adès'
Concerto Piano and Orchestra in 2019, Gerstein has performed the work over 50
times, with 20 different orchestras on three continents. Gerstein also
recently recorded Thomas Larcher's Piano Concerto with the Bergen Philharmonic
and Ed Gardner for ECM. In the 2025/26 season, he will give the première of a
new concerto written for him by Spanish composer, Francisco Coll. Gerstein is
dedicated to learning. He is currently Professor of Piano at Berlin's Hanns
Eisler Hochschule and on the faculty of Kronberg Academy. At Kronberg Academy,
his series of free online seminars featuring conversations with the 21st
Century's leading artistic minds has to date reached an audience of over
150,000 viewers. Gerstein also coaches at the Verbier Festival Academy and at
IMS Prussia Cove.
