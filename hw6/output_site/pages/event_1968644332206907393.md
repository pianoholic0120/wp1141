##  節目介紹

  

  

絳

  
****

絳，大赤也。

記憶，是一抹紅。是與肉身離散後，重拾失落記憶中的他方。





一縷紅，殘墨痕，

驚夢初醒，又再度墜入迷津。

稜角破映，星塵在隙縫間漫流，

沒有入口，也沒有盡頭，

將一切沉沒，又將一切托舉。

踮起腳尖，行走在影子失落的未至之境，

攪動夢的形狀，晝夜失去刻度。

漣漪迴旋，肉身離散，

拾鐲，繫鎖，拾起夢的碎片，將身影託付彼岸。



**《絳》策展介紹**



世紀當代舞團自10月至12月，於「脊地艸台」結合靜態文物展示、舞蹈肢體展演、觀眾裝扮體驗，重新編織過去、現在與未來的時光紋理。

10月（10/3-12/26）以靜態文物展揭幕，解鎖過往被塵埃封印的清代文物，每週輪流展出清代女性服裝、銀飾、生活器物等，梳理審美流變與工藝脈絡。結合不定期行為演出，彷彿走進百年前的女子閨閣，感受「生活在文物」的時空置換。



11月(11/1-11/30）的舞蹈展演，由編舞家姚淑芬攜手日本藝術家高橋啟祐的影像設計，以靜默文物交織舞蹈肢體。影像流光在舞動間綻裂，擾動時間紋理，潮汐吐納，蝶影幻化，如四季生滅輪迴。舞者重現清代女性裝扮儀式，衣著披掛、妝容描繪、飾品配戴，在寐與醒之間堆疊傳統與當代。



12月（12/5-12/26）(節目取消)每場次只限1-2位觀眾入場，成為裝扮儀式的主角，走進穿越古今的試衣間，身影端坐，如待續畫卷。對應不同年齡與身份形象，在舞者的協助下梳化裝扮，穿上繡花旗鞋，緩步搖曳。透過3D列印掃描，觀眾的身影被捕捉、重組，留存於虛擬光影，如鏡中對望，古今疊映，體驗一場閨閣夢境。

### **Crimson**

**Crimson (絳)** — a deep red.

**Memory** is a stroke of red — a recollection from elsewhere, retrieved after
the body has dispersed.

  

A wisp of red,  
faint traces of ink,  
from the shock of dreams we awaken, only to fall once more into the maze.  
Sharp angles reflect, as stardust flows through the cracks,  
with no entrance, no end,  
sinking all, yet lifting all.  
On tiptoes, we tread where shadows have not yet reached,  
stirring the shape of dreams, while day and night lose their measure.  
Ripples spiral, the body dissolves.  
Bracelets are found, locks are tied,  
fragments of dreams are gathered,  
and the silhouette is entrusted to the far shore.

### **About the Exhibition: "Crimson (絳)"**

From October to December, Century Contemporary Dance Company presents
**“Crimson”** at **Jidi CaoTai** , an immersive experience that fuses static
artifact exhibitions, contemporary dance performances, and participatory
dressing experiences — reweaving the textures of time across past, present,
and future.

* * *

### **October (10/3–12/30): Artifact Exhibition**

Opening with a static exhibition of **Qing Dynasty artifacts** , long sealed
in dust, the space reveals a rotation of items including **women’s garments,
silver ornaments, and daily objects** — tracing aesthetic shifts and the
lineage of craftsmanship.

Intermittent performance art brings the past to life — as if stepping into the
boudoir of a woman from centuries ago, inviting the audience to experience a
**temporal dislocation where life dwells within the artifact**.

### **November (11/1–11/30): Dance Performance & Exhibition**

The **dance performance** , choreographed by **Yao Shufen** in collaboration
with Japanese visual artist **Keisuke Takahashi** , interweaves silent
artifacts with expressive movement.

Projected visuals shimmer and fracture through dance, disturbing the texture
of time — like tides inhaling and exhaling, butterflies in metamorphosis,
seasons in endless cycle.

Dancers reenact **Qing Dynasty dressing rituals** : donning traditional
attire, applying makeup, adorning silver jewelry — layering tradition and
contemporaneity in a space between waking and dreaming.

### **December (12/5–12/26): Dressing Experience & Interactive Installation**

Each session allows **only 1–2 audience members** to become the protagonists
in a **transformative dressing ritual**. Step into a time-traveling fitting
room — where your image sits as a living scroll awaiting its continuation.

In collaboration with performers, participants are styled according to
different ages and identities, donning embroidered qipaos and delicately
walking in flowered shoes.

Using **3D scanning and digital reconstruction** , each silhouette is captured
and preserved in virtual light and shadow — a mirrored encounter across time,
where the ancient and modern coalesce in a **dreamlike chamber**.

  * 如購票時使用文化幣或點數折抵，退票時，系統將退還折抵之全額文化幣，並於扣除退票手續費後，優先退還點數，再將剩餘款項退回。特別提醒，如退票時（含換、補票及因節目異動之退票），抵用之文化幣、點數已逾使用效期，OPENTIX將無法以任何形式進行返還、展延。

  * 【優惠組合退票】若購買兩個品項以上之優惠組合，退票須全數辦理退票，或退票後剩餘數量仍符合原優惠組合規則，方可辦理。

  * 【套票退票】套票退票需整套辦理，無法以單張票券申請退票，亦不接受換票。如購買不同場次票券，最遲需於所購買的首場演出日10日前（不含演出日）辦理，如套票另有規定者，請依套票頁面之注意事項為主。
